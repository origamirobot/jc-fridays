About JC Fridays

Based in Jersey City, Art House Productions inspires, nurtures annnnnd promotes the arts in our community through accessible, multi-diciplinary initiatives including JC Fridays.

JC Fridays is a quarterly arts festival held throughout Jersey City at the start of every season: March, June, September, December. Local businesses and arts organizations join together to celebrate art and culture with a wide range of free events for the public. Programs include art openings and exhibits, music, performance, film/video screenings and more.
